What I Do

Privacy Rights

Opt-outs, Access, Deletion

Facilitating data subject rights across multiple jurisdictions — GDPR (EU), CPRA (California), PIPEDA (Canada) — including DSAR management, consent withdrawal, and right-to-erasure programs at scale.

Compliance

Building Privacy Programs

Privacy Notices, Cookie Audits, GPC Compliance, Records of Processing Activities (ROPAs) and Data Privacy Impact Assessments (DPIAs). End-to-end programs designed to protect your organization and the people it serves. Advising on the technical and non-technical implementation of handling sensitive personal data.

Audits

Data Privacy Audits

Questionnaires, gap analyses, and continuous monitoring of organization data flows. Identifying and mitigating privacy risks — including Legitimate Interest Assessments (LIA) and Vendor Privacy Assessments — before they become incidents.

AI Governance

Responsible AI in Practice

Experienced leader and contributor to AI Governance — bridging multidisciplinary teams, regulatory compliance, Privacy by Design, and real-world AI deployment across business units.

Product

Privacy by Design

Embedding privacy into every product sprint. Conducting DPIAs, advising engineering teams on data minimization and consent architecture, and ensuring features ship compliant — from kickoff to launch.

Vendor Management

Third-Party Onboarding

Drafting and negotiating Data Processing Agreements (DPAs), SaaS contracts, Marketing Agreements, and Service Agreements — ensuring every third-party vendor meets privacy and compliance requirements before they touch your data.
